# ezGym Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-05

## What ezGym does NOT collect
- No personal information
- No workout data leaves your device
- No analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs
- No crash reports, no telemetry
- No advertising identifiers

## What ezGym stores
- Your workouts, exercises, and preferences are stored locally on your iPhone using standard iOS storage. They never leave your device.

## In-app purchases
- ezGym uses Apple's StoreKit to process the one-time unlock purchase. Apple manages this transaction; ezGym never sees your payment information.

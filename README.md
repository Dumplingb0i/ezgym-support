## About
ezGym is a set counter and rest timer for lifters. No subscriptions — one-time purchase.

## Common Questions

**How do I restore my purchase on a new device?**
Open the app, tap the "+" on the workouts list, then tap "Restore Purchases" on the paywall. Your unlock is tied to your Apple ID.

**Rest timer isn't ringing when my phone is locked. What do I do?**
iPhone Settings → Notifications → ezGym → make sure Allow Notifications is on and Time Sensitive Notifications is enabled. On Focus modes (Sleep, Work, DND), the whistle still fires thanks to time-sensitive notifications, but silent mode still mutes it.

**How do I request a feature or report a bug?**
Email roprea77@gmail.com — I read every message.

## Privacy
ezGym stores your workouts locally on your device. No data is sent to any server. No analytics. No tracking. Nothing leaves your phone.
